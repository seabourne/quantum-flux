emit()

Overwrite EventEmitter.prototype so that our `emit` method is integrated into the Dispatcher instances dispatch-phase. I.e., we don't want to emit anything until after the dispatch-phase is complete & all stores have processed the current payload. Because all emits are synchronous, we don't have to worry about our async dispatcher
